  5. After sending your order form, invoice will be sent to your email within 24-48 hours.

AFTER ORDERING

  1. Please be patient. All items are pre-order unless stated that on-hand. ETA is just a time frame. We will be using SEA CARGO for Normal ETA, so it may take a while. 3-4 weeks if no delays after shipment from KR/TH/JP. 1-2 weeks if no delays if items are from PH.

  3. You can check updates on our masterlist.

PAYMENT

  1. There will be 2 rounds of payment, first will be the item price, 2nd is for the shipping fee/handling fee.
  2. Be mindful of the deadline of order (DOO) and the deadline of payment (DOP) for all the group orders.
  3. Kindly message us on or before DOP if you have payment concerns. A penalty of Php 30/day will be charged with failure of payment on the given DOP.
  4. Always fill out the payment form after sending your payment. We will not acknowledge your payment if you do not have record with our payment form.
  5. Expect that your payment will be reflected on our masterlist within 3 days. Kindly message us if the status did not change after the said days.
  6. Bogus buyers will be posted.
